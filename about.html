Our Mission

At ADCHASE, we aim to bridge the connection between consumers and outdoor advertising agencies.

By providing promotional material on public display advertising and geo-tracking on data, ADCHASE allows consumers to earn money by helping others save money and aids advertising agencies with increasing their sales.



Founded in January 2020 by three fellows at Diverse Influencers who wanted to change the game for outdoor advertisers, ADCHASE is a data analytics startup company located in NYC. Seeking a way for both consumers and advertising agencies to benefit from the bustling public transit in the city, ADCHASE offers a shopping app and analytics to maximize value in consumers' carts and outdoor advertisements.

Users

Can users exchange promotional deals?

Yes! ADCHASE has a trading system in which users can automatically trade promotions they find based on interests.

What kinds of products does ADCHASE promote?

Several products are promoted on ADCHASE's platform. We range from fashion to food, electronics, sports, and more. ADCHASE continuously updates its database with the latest deals.

Are other users able to see my interests?

Users can view each other's interests throughout the platform. However, users can adjust their privacy settings from public to friends only.
Users also have the option to trade publicly or friends only as well.





Advertising Agencies

Where are advertisements optimized?

ADCHASE currently targets outdoor advertisements primarily in the subway stations of NYC. We also have advertisements on digital rooftop displays of taxis and billboards throughout the city.

How much does ADCHASE charge for cost per click?

ADCHASE charges 10 cents per click.

Does ADCHASE take commission on product sales?

Yes. ADCHASE makes a 15% commission on sales below $100, a 10% commission on sales between $100 and $999, and 5% commission on sales above $1,000.
